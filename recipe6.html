Loaded Twice Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Bake potatoes for 1 hour until tender.
  2. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mix potato flesh with sour cream, butter, salt, and pepper. Stir in half of the cheese.
  4. Spoon mixture back into potato skins. Top with remaining cheese and bacon.
  5. Return to oven and bake for 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve warm.

These Loaded Twice Baked Potatoes are a comfort food classic, perfect for any meal or gathering!
