Homemade Savory Cashew Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 cup roasted cashews
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add ginger, bell peppers, and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch in a bowl. Mix well.
  5. Return chicken to the skillet, add the sauce mixture, and cook until thickened.
  6. Add cashews, mix well, and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

This easy and flavorful Cashew Chicken will become a household favorite. Quick, savory, and delightful!
