Mint Chip Cheesecake Mousse

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 3-4 drops green food coloring
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add powdered sugar and continue beating until well combined.
  3. Stir in peppermint extract and food coloring, mixing until fully blended.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until light and fluffy.
  6. Stir in mini chocolate chips.
  7. Spoon mousse into serving glasses and chill for at least 1 hour.
  8. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings before serving.

This Mint Chip Cheesecake Mousse is creamy, refreshing, and perfect for mint chocolate lovers. A delightful dessert for any occasion!
